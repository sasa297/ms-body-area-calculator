Fabrication calculator for enclosure design

Overview

This tool helps estimate overall weight and fabrication cost based on dimensions and material thickness.

Features

  • Input dimensions (L × W × H)
  • Single/Twin/Double door options
  • Auto-calculated area and weight
  • Fabrication rate and cost estimation
